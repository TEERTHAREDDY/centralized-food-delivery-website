Welcome to Our Campus Dining System

Our centralized food delivery platform was designed to solve a major problem faced by university students: long waiting times, limited eatery hours, and inconsistent meal service across campus eateries. We bring all on-campus dining options into one powerful, easy-to-use system.

What Problems We Solve

  • Long Queues: Eliminate time wasted waiting in lines for food pickup.
  • Limited Access: Expand food availability beyond traditional cafeteria hours.
  • Manual Management: Shift from error-prone manual ordering to seamless digital experiences.
  • Lack of Tracking: Provide real-time status updates on food preparation and delivery.
  • Meal Plan Integration: Support easy payment through student meal plans or online options.

How Our System Works

  • Student Registration: Students register and log into the centralized portal.
  • Browse Menus: View real-time menus from multiple on-campus eateries.
  • Order Placement: Quickly place orders for pickup or delivery using a simple form.
  • Order Tracking: Monitor real-time updates on order preparation, delivery status, and more.
  • Feedback and Support: Submit feedback and queries directly through the platform.

Our Impact on Campus

Since introducing this system, students have experienced reduced wait times by over 70%, improved meal satisfaction scores, and easier access to healthy food options. Eateries manage orders more efficiently, and meal plan usage has increased due to the ease of integration.

Key Features

  • Dynamic real-time menus and order updates.
  • Meal plans and external payment support.
  • Secure login and student profile management.
  • Order history and reordering in one click.
  • Personalized delivery and pickup options based on campus locations.
