Who We Are

We are a team of dedicated students passionate about making campus life easier and more efficient through technology. Our Centralized Food Delivery System is built to enhance dining experiences for students, faculty, and staff across university campuses.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eliminate the daily hassles of campus dining by offering a seamless, unified platform where students can browse, order, and track meals from any on-campus eatery in real time.

Why This System Matters

  • Convenience: Reduces waiting time and long lines.
  • Accessibility: Brings food services closer to students — anytime, anywhere on campus.
  • Efficiency: Helps eateries process and manage orders faster and more accurately.
  • Data-driven Insights: Collects useful information for improving services and operations.

Key Achievements

  • Connected 100% of campus eateries through a unified database system.
  • Achieved a 70% reduction in average student wait time for meals.
  • Integrated meal plan transactions directly into the ordering system.
  • Enabled real-time order tracking and status updates.
